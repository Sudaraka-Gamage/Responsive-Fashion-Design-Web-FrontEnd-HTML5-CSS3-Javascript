‘We are The Vanta Studios. Come get some.’

A international fashion brand, owned by leading Sri Lankan apparel manufacturer. This brand started its journey in retailing in June of 2010 throughout the Asia-Pacific region. Launched itself into the biggest department store in Colombo, Sri Lanka. With time, the brand, Vanta, started its own retail business with huge Flagship Store of approximately 2700 square feet, in Colombo and gradually opened 5 more stores across Sri Lanka and 12 in India as well.
